Our Mission

Empowering the next generation of product leaders at HEC Paris

1.

Build Product Thinkers

Develop strong product sense and strategic thinking among MBA candidates by offering hands-on training, real-world problem-solving, and structured PM preparation.

2.

Accelerate PM Careers

Equip HEC MBA students with the skills, frameworks, and confidence needed to successfully navigate the Product Management recruiting process.

3.

Foster PM Community

Create a collaborative network across cohorts, enabling peer learning, focused discussions, and meaningful connections for students aspiring to careers in product.

What is a Product Manager?

Quick overview before diving into the full guide

Core Question

"What problem are we solving and for whom?"

Key Responsibilities

Define roadmap, prioritize features, align stakeholders, ship products

Daily Work

User interviews, PRDs, prioritization, meetings with eng & design

Success Metrics

Revenue, adoption, engagement, customer satisfaction
